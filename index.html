Redefining Sustainability in Tech

Project EcoPhone represents a complete shift from a linear lifecycle model to a modular, circular, and highly resilient smartphone architecture.

Discover the Redesign

Product Concept

The Problem

Traditional smartphones utilize hidden industrial glues, programmed obsolescence, and proprietary hardware locking methods, contributing massively to global e-waste and carbon emissions.

The Solution

EcoPhone focuses on a purely mechanical modular framework, widespread standardization of parts, bio-based resilient chassis materials, and internal components designed specifically for a 7-year life span.

Improved Design Mechanics

🌱

Eco-Materials

Back casing molded with PCR-PC and hemp fibers. Mid-frame utilizes 100% recycled structural aluminum (AL 7075-R).

🔋

Energy Efficiency

Smart PMIC with aggressive power gating and 1Hz LTPO OLED decreases daily power draw completely by 30%.

🧩

Modular Repair

Pogo-pin connector interface replaces soldered ribbon cables, enabling swift snap-in module replacements without heat.

⏳

Lifespan Extension

Because component failure is untethered from the whole unit, the standard smartphone lifespan pushes from 2.5 to 7 years.

CAD Structural Assembly

Disassembly-Friendly Architecture

  • Standardized Fasteners: M1.5 Torx (T5) used globally across the device for one-tool repair.
  • Snap-Fit Screen: Embedded tabs release via standard spudger. Zero heat guns.
  • Replaceable Battery Basket: Uses a mechanical tension basket with a built-in 3-second pull lever.
  • Hexagonal Ribbing: 18% structural weight reduction achieved via advanced aluminum honeycomb mid-frame meshing.
